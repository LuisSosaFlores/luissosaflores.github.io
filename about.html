About

Luis Sosa works at the intersection of AI and automation. Alongside building a career as a data scientist and data analyst, Luis has helped organisations and small teams develop a go-to-market strategy based on reducing rework and automating monotonous tasks. He helps founders and entrepreneurs launch their products and services, even when they have few or no resources.

He earned his Master's degree in Data Science & Business Analytics at KU Leuven in Belgium, having received the "Master Mind" excellence scholarship.

Having built networks in Mexico, the US, Belgium, Portugal and the Netherlands, he now lives in Freiburg im Breisgau, Germany's green capital.

Outside of work, Luis spends his time learning about psychology, cognition and behavioural science. He is also eager to advance the state of the science by conducting research in the field of learning, with the aim of helping people learn better with support from technologies such as AI and NLP.

If you would like to chat about common interests or potential collaborations, please get in touch.
